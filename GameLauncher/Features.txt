(Mini Game Launcher)
[Features]
  (Drag and Drop Exes of Games/Programs or Shortcuts Directly onto Gui To add them.)
  (Clicking the Game Icon Runs the Game/Program.)
  (Right Clicking the Name on the Gui allows you to Rename incase you changed your mind from what you named it.)
  (Right Clicking a blank space on the gui allows for the creation of a New Category that when opened will
   open another instance of the launcher allowing you to add up to 15 programs/games per New Categories)
  (15 Maximum Games/Programs at the moment.)
  (Right Clicking the Icon Opens an Options Menu.)
   [Options]
     (Delete the Game/Program from the Gui.)
     (Run the game with Additional Parameters.)
